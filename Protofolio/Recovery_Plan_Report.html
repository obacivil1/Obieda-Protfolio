1 Recovery Plan Executive Summary

The Zone Residential Complex is currently 45 days behind the baseline schedule (SPI = 0.92, ES-based delay of ~20 days). This Recovery Plan outlines a structured intervention program targeting 25 days of net schedule recovery by Q1 2023 at an estimated cost of $425,000, yielding a projected ROI of 4.2x against potential liquidated damages of $1.8M.

2 Recovery Measures — Detailed

1. Workforce Augmentation 18 days recovery
Mobilize 44 additional workers: 32 finishing laborers (increase from 88 to 120) and 12 facade installers (increase from 28 to 40). Workers sourced from Eastern Province with 2-week mobilization period. Estimated cost: $140K (mobilization, accommodation, productivity ramp-up). ROI: 7.7x against LD exposure.
2. Night Shift Operations 12 days recovery
Implement night shift for critical finishing activities: plastering, tiling, ceiling installation. 6 PM — 2 AM shift with dedicated supervision and lighting. Target: 40% of finishing workforce on night shift. Estimated cost: $95K (shift premium, lighting, supervision). ROI: 7.6x
3. Material Expediting 14 days recovery
Pre-order facade aluminum composite panels with air freight for 40% of critical items. Vessel lead time: 6 weeks → air freight: 2 weeks. Order placed directly with factory, bypassing local agent. Estimated cost: $85K (air freight premium). ROI: 9.9x
4. BIM Coordination & Rework Reduction 8 days recovery
Weekly BIM coordination sessions between structural, MEP, and finishing trades. Model clash detection before physical installation. Target: reduce MEP rework by 60%. Estimated cost: $12K. ROI: 40x
5. Sequence Re-optimization 6 days recovery
Re-sequence finishing packages to enable parallel execution where possible. Levels 1-4 tiling to commence before ceiling installation in Levels 5-8. Update P6 logic ties. Estimated cost: $18K (planning effort). ROI: 20x
6. QA/QC Fast-Track 6 days recovery
Dedicated QA/QC inspector assigned to finishing and facade packages. Same-day inspection approvals instead of 2-3 day standard cycle. Estimated cost: $18K. ROI: 20x

Recovery Risk Buffer

A risk buffer of 8 days is included in the recovery plan. The target of 25 days' recovery is calculated on a P50 basis. The P80 (80% confidence) recovery is estimated at 18 days. The P20 (optimistic) recovery is 32 days. Monthly recovery reviews will track actual vs. planned progress and allow course correction.

Preventive Recommendations for Future Phases

1. Resource-Loaded Schedule Validation
All tender schedules must include resource loading (labor by trade, by month). Validate against historical productivity norms before contract award.
2. Design Freeze Gate
Implement a formal design freeze milestone at IFC stage. Any post-freeze changes to go through a schedule/cost impact assessment gate with Q Design and Takamul sign-off.
3. Monthly Resource Forecast
Monthly resource forecasting for the next 3 months — identify shortfalls 8 weeks ahead. Use P6 resource analysis as an early warning tool.
