Delay Event Timeline

Day 45 — Feb 2024

Design Approval Delay

Late issuance of architectural drawing approvals by consultant caused 28-day delay to foundation works commencement.

Day 98 — Apr 2024

Utility Relocation

Existing underground utility lines not identified in contract documents required redesign and relocation, impacting site preparation by 35 days.

Day 156 — Jun 2024

Material Procurement Delay

Steel reinforcement supply chain disruption due to global market conditions caused 22-day delay to superstructure works.

Day 210 — Aug 2024

Weather / Force Majeure

Exceptional rainfall events (above historical average) halted exterior works for 18 days across two separate events.

Day 298 — Nov 2024

MEP Coordination

Clash between HVAC ductwork and fire protection systems required redesign coordination, impacting Zone B completion by 15 days.

Day 380 — Feb 2025

Client Variations

Additional architectural finishes requested by client for ground floor retail areas — 30-day impact approved via variation order.

Impact Analysis

Critical Path Impact

Delay to foundation and superstructure activities directly impacted the project critical path, extending the overall completion date by 187 days.

Cost Impact

Total cost impact estimated at $1.85M including extended site overheads, escalation, and disruption costs. Employer-responsible delays account for ~$1.1M.

Mitigation Measures

Recovery schedule implemented with 35-day compression through overtime, additional resources, and sequence optimization. Target recovery: 45% of total delay.

Recommendations

Early design freeze protocol, weekly coordination meetings, and buffer management system recommended for future project phases.
