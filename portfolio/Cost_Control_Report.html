Favorable -$160K

Structural — Concrete Savings

Concrete unit price secured at $85/m³ vs. budgeted $92/m³ due to bulk procurement agreement. Total savings on 16,500 m³ poured to date.

Favorable -$40K

MEP — Competitive Tender

MEP subcontract awarded 4.5% below budget. Efficient material procurement from direct suppliers. HVAC ductwork 6% under budget.

Adverse +$30K

Finishing — Labor Escalation

Adverse +$22K
